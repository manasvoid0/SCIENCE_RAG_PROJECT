I always found photosynthesis really interesting. Basically plants make their own food, which is kind of amazing if you think about it. My teacher told us the green colour in leaves comes from chlorophyll. That part catches the sunlight. Then the plant takes in water from the roots and carbon dioxide from the air. I think the sunlight helps mix them together to make a sugar called glucose. The plant uses this sugar for energy to grow taller and stronger. One cool thing is that oxygen comes out as a leftover, and that is the air we breathe. For example, the big tree outside my house is basically cleaning the air for us every day. So yeah, photosynthesis is super important and honestly underrated.
